Sinner's Sandwich

Description

This wholesome sandwich comes from a little diner in GreenVale, a real treat an far from shy on it's flavor profile.

"Self afflicting punishment to atone for past sins" FM

Ingredients

  • Sandwich Bread
  • Turkey
  • Strawberry Jam
  • Cereal
  • Mayonaise

Steps

  1. You can either toast the bread or not depending on your preference.
  2. Warm the turkey on a pan.
  3. Place mayo on both pieces of bread.
  4. Place a generous amount of strawberry jam on one piece of bread.
  5. Place cereal on top of the strawberry jam.
  6. Place the turkey on the other piece of bread.
  7. Add salt and pepper if needed.
  8. Close the sandwich and enjoy!
