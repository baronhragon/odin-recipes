Lasagna

Description

This recipe is not the usual lasagna recipe. This recipe is an alternative version for people that are looking for a tomato-less version of the classic lasagna.

This recipe resembles more of a Mousaka, which is a lasagna like recipe from greece that uses vegetables for layers, instead of pasta. It's worth trying out if you feel adventurous.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Chicken
  • Bay Leaf
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Ricotta

Steps

  1. Start by putting a pot of water to boil in order to put the lasagna pasta in, put some salt in the water.
  2. Finely cut onion and garlic.
  3. Put the onion and garlic on a hot pan with olive oil and cook for 2 minutes or until onion is tender.
  4. Throw a pad of butter in the pan and let it melt.
  5. Add flour to the pan and mix well with the butter.
  6. Start adding milk and stir well to avoid lumps.
  7. Add bay leaf, salt and pepper to the sauce.
  8. Check for taste, if it's to your liking, remove from heat and let cool.
  9. Once the water for the pasta starts to boil, add the pasta and stir for 2 minutes so it doesn't stick together.
  10. Let pasta cook in water for 8 minutes.
  11. Remove pasta from water and cool under running water until room temperature.
