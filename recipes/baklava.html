Baklava

Description

Baklavas are a middle eastern dessert that's pretty simple but delicious. Normally they are acompanied by coffee or tea.

They may seem difficult to make, but they are far from it.

Ingredients

  • Phillo pastry
  • Butter
  • Walnuts
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter on a pan.
  2. Carefully unroll the phillo pastry on a work surface.
  3. Layer 4 sheets of phillo pastry on top of each other placing melted butter between each sheet.
  4. Place a generous amount of ricotta in a log form in the middle of the phillo sheets.
  5. Place walnuts on top of the ricotta and start rolling the phillo pastry into itself.
  6. Once you completely roll the phillo, grab a knife and cut into 6 inch logs.
  7. Roll each log onto itself to make a spiral.
  8. Preheat the oven at 350*F
  9. Drizzle more butter on top of the baklavas and some more walnuts.
  10. Place in the oven for 20 min.
  11. On a pot, place equal amounts of sugar and water.
  12. Bring the sugar/water mix to a boil.
  13. Once the baklavas are ready to go out from the oven, drench them with the syrup.
